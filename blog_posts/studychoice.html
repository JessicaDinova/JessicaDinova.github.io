Study choice



After my graduation in my High School I wanted to put my programming skills to another level. After all I loved and enjoyed the process of programming new things and my long-term dream was to be a programmer. But there was a catch.



My home country – Slovakia is not developed in such a way I wanted it to be. What was new in programming in Slovakia was considered old and unnecessarily complicated everywhere else. Therefore I applied for HZ university in Netherlands, away from my family and friends. While I’m going to study here I am hoping and expecting to learn how to program at the highest level, how to be part of a team or how to lead one.


After completing my studies I would love to work in videogame industry as a developer or even a designer. After all I was always fascinated by the beauty of videogames – their environment, storylines, characters and animations.
