My thoughts



Feedback is really extensive and constructed in positive manners. Mrs. Daphne really dive into it and wanted to guide me onto the right path. I can disagree because I said that I don’t like to work with people but that’s where the communication failed and we did not understand each other. It’s true that I don’t like to work with them but I did not specified which part.

Overall I really enjoyed to read this feedback and I mainly concentrated on things that I can improve so that I could become better person.
