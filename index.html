Welcome,

My name is Jessica, student at HZ university of applied sciences in Netherlands. Thank you for visiting my website

At my worst, the only things that could get me through were videogames. I suddenly felt great, my brain was only concentrated on the game. Therefore I applied to my former high school where I could see what was hiding behind these games – code. There I’ve managed to learn some programming languages such as:

  • C++
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • SQL

But I wanted more. I wanted to make people get through their hard times in life via videogames. As my form or graduation I had the opportunity to create my own application that would determine my final grade. I’ve chosen to make an app to help you study and remember your important definition of words.
But tha wasn't enough for me and I’ve applied for the ICT program here in HZ. I know that it’s hard to learn new programming languages but I have to keep pushing so that I can achieve my long-term dream – to create videogames. In addition I get to learn even more then how to create them.
And as we say in Slovakia “z každého rožka troška” - in translation “a little from every corner” I can be educated in multiple things at the same time – which had never hurt anyone.
