SWOT analysis



For those who don’t know what a personal SWOT analysis is let me explain. SWOT stands for (S- strengths, W- weaknesses, O– opportunities, T- threats), you make a simple table with these columns and write a list of your qualities. After you are done you can briefly see what kind of a person you are and how can you improve yourself or how to solve your problems.


My SWOT analysis really uncovered my “dark sides” and after a feedback from my professor I know how to improve them so that they won’t bother me anymore.


Strenghts Weakness
Critical thinking
I can lead people
Good communication skills
I can learn very quickly		 I get angry all the time
Sometimes I can be lazy
I don't like to be surrounded by many people (it makes me anxious)
Opportunities Threats
Because I can learn quickly I can use my new knowledge to improve some of my projects
With my leading skills I am able to guide a team of people so that the project is done as soon as possible
If I can manage my anger I can find solution much quicker		 I lose motivations when I am surrounded by many people
My anger often leads to difficulties with other people
If I am not enjoying my job I get lazy and won't finish the job as I was intending to
